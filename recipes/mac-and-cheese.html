Baked Macaroni and Cheese

  • 200 g Macaroni
  • 1 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Salt, Pepper, Basil
  • Olive Oil

    1. Boil the macaroni in salted water
    2. Drain; mix with olive oil salt, pepper and basil
    3. Add tomato sauce
    4. Add pumpkin seeds
    5. Add the grated parmesan cheese
    6. Stir everything well
    7. Bake in the oven in a glass dish for 20 min at 200 degrees
